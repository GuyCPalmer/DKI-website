About Deborah

A home shaped by
the way you live.

Every project begins with you. Who lives here? What do you love? How does life move through these rooms? The answers shape a space that feels entirely, unmistakably yours.

A thread of antiques has run through every chapter of Deborah's career — from buying trips with her mother, to apprenticing at Keller Henderson Interiors in New York, to helping design Camp Lucy in the Texas Hill Country.

"The juxtaposition of architecturals and furnishings with new and familiar gathered objects gives such warmth to a space."
Deborah Kirk · Principal Designer

Services

Every detail considered,
every room lived in.
Whether starting from the ground up or reimagining an existing home, each project is guided by a commitment to comfort, craft, and the stories that objects carry.

01

Full-Service Design

From concept through installation — space planning, material selection, furniture sourcing, and project management to a beautifully finished home.

02

Renovation & Remodel

Thoughtful redesigns that honor the bones of a home while creating rooms that feel new, intentional, and entirely yours.

03

Antique Curation

Sourcing and integrating antiques, architecturals, and gathered objects that give each room depth, warmth, and a sense of story.

04

New Construction

Collaborating with architects and builders from the ground up to ensure every interior decision is made with intention and coherence.

05

Commercial Spaces

Designing hospitality, retail, and event venues — including ranch properties and wedding destinations — that leave a lasting impression.

06

Design Consultation

A focused session for homeowners who need expert guidance on a specific space, color direction, or furniture arrangement.

How we work

A clear path from first meeting
to finished room.

01

Discovery Call

Book a free 30 minute conversation to understand your vision, budget and timeline.

02

Site Walkthrough

An in-person visit to understand your space - how it feels, how it flows and how you live in it.

03

Concept & Sourcing

Every selection is made with your space and your life in mind — materials, furnishings, and finishes chosen specifically for you, not pulled from a catalogue.

04

Design Presentation

We present the full concept for your review — every selection explained, every decision aligned. Work begins only once you're completely on board.

05

Installation

Full project management through delivery and installation, so you arrive to a finished, styled home.
